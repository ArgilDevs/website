ARGIL URL Shortener

Shorten Your Own Links

I made my own link shortener using a GitHub issue page as a database

You can shorten your own links @ the 'Database' all you have to do is add the FULL long links as the title of a new issue (the link makes a new one for you), including the 'http(s)://' at the start! Sometimes it's a bit buggy, but you can easily fix most of those issues, a fix I found is just adding 'www' after the 'http(s)://' To access your shortened links just use https://argil.dev/{issue_number}
